About Pandora

Building at the intersection of blockchain and real-world value.

Founded around 2021, Pandora Finance set out with a stubborn belief: the most valuable things in the world — property, collectibles, precious metals, everyday commerce — still live off-chain, illiquid and hard to reach. So we build the rails to bring them on.

From that thesis grew a family of products. Aconomy — our flagship real-world asset marketplace and the rebrand of our earliest work — sits alongside Propty, Express Protocol, The DPP Company and UnityMarket. Same studio, same mission, expressed across markets.

  • 2021Pandora Finance is foundedA studio to connect blockchain with real-world value.
  • 2022UnityMarket & early NFT betsFirst experiments in onchain ownership and collectibles.
  • 2023Aconomy takes shapePi-NFTs, asset validators and a testnet for tokenizing RWAs.
  • NowAn expanding product familyAconomy, Propty and Express Protocol pushing onchain into daily life.
