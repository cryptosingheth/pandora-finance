Watch two agents settle a purchase on their own

A live simulation of the x402 flow on Express Protocol. A buyer-agent discovers a listing, a seller-agent quotes it, and payment clears over HTTP 402 in USDC — then the asset transfers onchain. No human at the checkout. Press run, or pick a scenario.

01 · discover

Find the listing

The buyer-agent calls discover_listing() and reads the asset, chain, and endpoint from the seller.

02 · quote

Price & terms

The seller-agent answers quote() with a price in USDC and settlement terms — no negotiation UI needed.

03 · x402 pay

HTTP 402 → 200

The endpoint returns 402 Payment Required; the buyer signs a USDC payment header and re-sends for 200 OK.

04 · settle

Transfer the asset

transfer_asset() fills the ERC-721 to the buyer's wallet. Both agents mark the trade settled autonomously.

Illustrative simulation only — no real wallets, funds, or onchain transactions. Balances, addresses, and token IDs are fictional and reset on each run. Built to show the shape of an x402 agent-to-agent settlement on the Express Protocol SDK.
