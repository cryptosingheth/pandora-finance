About Pandora

An AI-native product company for real-world commerce.

Pandora Finance is the brand of Aconomy Labs Inc., a Canadian technology company founded around 2021. We're a product company and venture studio: we build AI-native software for real-world commerce, and we own the products we ship. Applied AI and agents do the work; on-chain infrastructure keeps it verifiable.

That approach runs across several domains. Aconomy — our flagship, an AI-assisted marketplace for real-world assets — sits alongside Propty, Express Protocol, The DPP Company and UnityMarket. One studio, one team, a portfolio of products we build and own.
