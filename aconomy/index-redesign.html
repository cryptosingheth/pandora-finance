How it works

From physical asset to on-chain liquidity in three steps.

For every NFT, there's a unique, real-life asset it's tied to. Here's how your asset makes the journey on-chain — and back again whenever you want.

STEP 01

Tokenize

Log in to your Aconomy profile and mint your real-world asset into an asset-backed NFT — with customizable attributes and everything you need to organize and manage your collection.

STEP 02

Validate

Reach a validator through our in-house messenger. They confirm authenticity and legitimacy using proof of ownership and provenance — and can lock collateral into the NFT, turning it into a Pi-NFT.

STEP 03

Trade, swap & lend

Our system's built for smooth sailing — swap your asset in a snap, use it as collateral for quick loans, or auction it. Boost liquidity and put your asset on the global stage.

What is a Pi-NFT?

More than a token — a real asset with real superpowers.

Each Pi-NFT is backed by a unique, specific physical collectible. That backing unlocks a set of capabilities ordinary NFTs simply don't have.
