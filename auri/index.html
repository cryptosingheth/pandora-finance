Good to know

Questions, answered.

Is Auri gold real gold?

Yes. Each unit equals one troy ounce of allocated bullion, held as a 1:1-backed gold token and independently audited.

Do I actually own it?

Always. You hold it in your own self-custody account — Auri never holds your assets.

Can I borrow without selling?

Yes — unlock cash against your gold up to ~77% LTV at ~3.75% APR, and keep the gold. KYC is required to buy with CAD, via regulated on/off-ramp partners.

Can an AI agent use Auri?

Yes — connect over MCP, or script it from the CLI/API. Agents can own, accumulate and borrow gold on your behalf, within scoped, revocable limits you set.
